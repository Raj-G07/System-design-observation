Unified Modeling Language (**UML**) is the **universal visual language** used to model, design, and document **object-oriented systems**.  
It bridges the gap between **High-Level Design (HLD)** and **Low-Level Design (LLD)** by visually representing **structure**, **behavior**, and **interactions**.

- Supports **OOP principles** like abstraction, encapsulation, and modularity.
